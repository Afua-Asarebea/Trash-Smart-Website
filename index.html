we are Trash Smart

Why We do What We do

Our aim is to help people who are frustrated by irregular and inconsistencies in their waste pick up schedules by allowing users to schedule and pick up waste based on their convenience.

Our vision is to ensure public health, create a clean environment and achieve environmental preservation through re-using, repairing, composting or recycling back into the system.

Our core values include professionalism, loyalty, commitment, respect and integrity.

It is our mission to be a company well known in communities for our provision of exceptional service by understanding the needs of our customers and creating convenience by helping create a clean environment.

Our Services

Pickups

Based on our packages for on-demand and subscribed. Pickups are done at your convinience. Available for households and organizations.

Waste Recycling

Collection of waste is done on scheduled dates and times per request of customers. The request made via the app and upon pickup new rubbers are installed in bins. Payment is cashless. We are available for one time pickup and subscription( weekly or monthly)
